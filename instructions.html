Instructions

Your Guide To Competing With Honor, Excellence, & Fair Play.

Rules & Regulations

01: 
Only players registered with the league are eligible to participate. Fielding unregistered players may result in match forfeiture.
02: 
All participants must adhere to strict safety protocols and the spirit of fair play. Violations can result in fines, or suspension.
03: 
Breaches of the league's rules may result in penalties ranging from fines to match suspensions, as determined by the league authorities.
04: 
All matches must be played at designated venues. Teams are required to adhere to all venue-specific regulations and guidelines.
05: 
In case of withdrawal or disqualification, only 20% of the entry fees will be refunded to the player.
06: 
If a match is postponed due to unforseen circumstances, such as weather conditions, it will be rescheduled on any other date, as prescribed by the league authorities.
07: 
League authorities reserve the right to change or adjust match schedules or formats as needed.

The EFL is a premier football competition designed to bring together young football enthusiasts from the local community to showcase their skills, foster teamwork and promote sportsmanship. It is structured to ensure fair play and provide an opportunity for players to gain exposure and experience in a competitive environment. Whether it is skillful dribbling, powerful shots or strategic teamwork, this tournament will be an exciting opportunity for the participants to grow and enjoy the beautiful game.
