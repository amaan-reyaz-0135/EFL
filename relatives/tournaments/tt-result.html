WELCOME TO THE TRILOGY TOURNAMENT!

On 02 February, 2025, Joggers Park became the battleground for the thrilling Trilogy Tournament, featuring a fierce rivalry between Thunder Bolts and Storm Breakers. The tournament followed a Best-Of-Three format, with each match lasting 30 minutes, creating a high-intensity showdown. Both teams paid an entry fee of ₹250, competing not just for pride but also for the ₹400 prize awaiting the winners.

The first match saw Storm Breakers come out strong, dominating the game with a commanding 6-2 victory, setting the tone for the series. However, the second match became an instant classic, as Thunder Bolts raced to a 6-1 lead, seemingly set to force a final decider. But Storm Breakers launched a breathtaking comeback, scoring six consecutive goals to take a 6-7 lead. Just when it seemed like they had sealed the win, Thunder Bolts struck late, snatching a dramatic 8-7 victory and pushing the tournament into a decisive third match.

Unfortunately, the final match was called off due to player unavailability from Thunder Bolts, leading to Storm Breakers being declared the winner of the tournament. Nabeel was the tournament's top scorer with 7 goals, while Tawheed and Sarhan both scored 4 goals each, playing crucial roles for their teams. Despite a rainy and slippery court, the players fought hard, though it led to Tawheed suffering a serious hand injury.

Though the tournament ended under unexpected circumstances, the Trilogy Tournament delivered breathtaking action, incredible resilience, and an unforgettable comeback story, making it a truly memorable event.

With the Trilogy Tournament setting the stage, excitement is already building for the next showdown. Bigger battles and intense rivalries await — Stay tuned for the next tournament!
