WELCOME TO THE TRILOGY TOURNAMENT!

We are excited to kick off this intense competition between two powerful teams; Thunder Bolts and Storm Breakers. Here's all the information you need to know before participation:

The tournament will be played in a best-of-three series format. The first team to win 2 matches will be crowned the winner of the Trilogy Tournament. The team with the most goals at the end of the 30 minute match will be declared the winner.

Each player must pay a registration fee of ₹50 to enter the tournament. The winning team will get a cash prize of ₹400 and losing team will get a cashback of ₹50.

Be sure to give your best because Champions keep playing until they get it right.
Let the game begin!
